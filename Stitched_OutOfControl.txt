Player has 1 party member: Their chimeric monster. 

Monster is a large beast made up of monster parts controlled by the player, a mad scientist at a monster research facility.
Gameplay loop: Battle > Loot > Stitch > Repeat

All enemies are monsters that can drop 1-3 body parts at end of battle from a potential pool of 3
Monster Parts: Can be stitched to the Chimera in one of 6 slots:

Head
Left Arm
Right Arm
Left Leg
Right Leg
Tail

Each of these slots can be filled with a monster part. Each monster part has a durability stat, which decrements every time a skill associated with that

Health - Total health pool
Strength - Modifier added to physical damage (+1 physical damage per point)
Intelligence - Modifier added to magic damage/effects (+1 magical damage per point)

Speed - Influences turn order + dodge chance (+5% dodge chance)
Defense - Reduces incoming physical damage (-1 physical damage per point)
Will - Reduces incoming magic damage (-1 magical damage per point)

Combat Flow:

Selection Phase:
Player sees their options as UI elements
List of Behaviors player can use

Pick one (Mark) > Target Select, one enemy sprite flashes (selected), player can change selection by using up/down commands, once they press 'ACCEPT' again, end Selection Phase and move to Execution Phase.

Execution Phase:
At start of Execution Phase: All non-player entities choose a behavior from their list of available behaviors (1-3 for enemies, more for the Chimera), and choose targets randomly.
Game calculates turn order based on speed rolls (all entities roll 1d3 + Speed)
In turn order, each character executes their action. If an entity is downed before its turn begins, it will not execute its action.

List of Status Effects:

Bind - Bound enemies have -15% dodge and +3 damage from attacks
